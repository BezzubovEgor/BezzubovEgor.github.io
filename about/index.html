About

I'm Yahor Bezzubau — a Staff Engineer at JetBrains, working on Kineto. Based in the Netherlands.

I've spent 7+ years building distributed systems, full-stack applications, and developer tools. My stack spans TypeScript, Go, Java, and Python — though these days I mostly think in TypeScript and Go. I care about architecture, developer experience, and building things that don't fall apart at scale.

Right now I'm deep in the world of AI agents — not the hype version, but the real engineering problems: how do you give agents persistent data? How do they manage permissions? What happens when three agents write to the same resource? The boring problems that make the magic possible.

  • TypeScript & Go — patterns, opinions, the stuff I learn building real systems
  • Platform engineering — APIs, protocols, developer tools
